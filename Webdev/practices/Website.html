Olivia Rodrigo

The Breakthrough

How Olivia Rodrigo, one of the most popular singers, rose to fame and stayed at the top of the Billboard charts with only two albums and around 30 songs over the last five years. Now she is about to launch her new album, “You Seem Pretty Sad for a Girl So in Love,” and everyone is going crazy. Let’s look at the breakthrough of this star — from being a child actor to becoming one of the top 50 artists in the world.

Being a Child Artist

Olivia Isabel Rodrigo was born in Temecula, California, U.S., on February 20, 2003. Her mother, Jenny Rodrigo, and her father, Chris Rodrigo, raised her in a loving home. Liv describes her childhood as wonderful. Since she was little, she had a passion for music, growing up listening to alternative rock bands like No Doubt, Pearl Jam, and Green Day. At an early age, she showed natural musical talent and took lessons to improve. Liv began to do music traning lessons at age 5, stared to play piano, participated in muscial theater, at Lisa J. Mails Elementary School in Murrieta. Liv also competed in local singing contests from a young age, Including the boys and girls Club idol, and stared attending acting lessons, giude by the advice of her vocal Coach.
