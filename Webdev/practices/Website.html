Olivia Rodrigo

The Breakthrough

How Olivia Rodrigo, one of the most popular singers, rose to fame and stayed at the top of the Billboard charts with only two albums and around 30 songs over the last five years. Now she is about to launch her new album, “You Seem Pretty Sad for a Girl So in Love” and everyone is going crazy. Let's look at the breakthrough of this star — from being a child actor to becoming one of the top 50 artists in the world.

Being a Child Artist

Olivia Isabel Rodrigo was born in Temecula, California, on February 20, 2003. She grew up listening to alternative rock bands like No Doubt, Pearl Jam, and Green Day. At age 5 she began music training, piano lessons, and musical theater. She also competed in local singing contests and later began acting lessons guided by her vocal coach.

Some of her first roles were:

  • Old Navy commercial (2014–2015)
  • An American Girl: Grace Stirs Up Success (2015) — Grace Thomas
  • Bizaardvark (2016–2019) — Paige Olvera
  • New Girl (2017) — Terrinea
  • High School Musical: The Musical: The Series (2019–2022) — Nini Salazar-Roberts
