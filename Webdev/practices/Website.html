Being a Child Artist

Olivia Isabel Rodrigo was born in Temecula, California, U.S., on February 20, 2003. Her mother, Jenny Rodrigo, and her father, Chris Rodrigo, raised her in a loving home. Liv describes her childhood as wonderful. Since she was little, she had a passion for music, growing up listening to alternative rock bands like No Doubt, Pearl Jam, and Green Day. At an early age, she showed natural musical talent and took lessons to improve. Liv began to do music traning lessons at age 5, stared to play piano, participated in muscial theater, at Lisa J.
